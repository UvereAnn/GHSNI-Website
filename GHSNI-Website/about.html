About Us.

This group was born out of quest to provide solutions to emerging health care problems. It is a nursing initiative to bring solutions to emerging health care problems and research methodology. This group is not all about being a graduate nurse or not but to us it is all about what we love to do. The passions we have to bring solutions to those problems we have seen affecting our patients, health care practitioners, communities and the world at large. It all about bringing out evidence based practice, quality, holistic and individualized health care.It is all about the passion to bring about effective change on our society.



CRITERIA FOR MEMBERSHIP:

Must be a graduate nurse, must have passion for productive change, willingness for active participation in research methodology
